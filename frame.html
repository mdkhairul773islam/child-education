Art with the Masters

Come make connections between art history, art appreciation, and most importantly art creation! Learn art through studying the masters in a variety of art genres and creating your own artwork to mimic the style!

Description

This class is all about making connections between art history, art appreciation, and most importantly art creation. Each week we will learn about a famous artist that has greatly influenced the art world. This class gives you a little taste of what we explore in greater depth in the Intro to Impro and Super Scriptwriting classes.
